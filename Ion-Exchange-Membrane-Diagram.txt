FLOW SUMMARY:
  Step 1: Pulverize ion exchange resin into dry powder
  Step 2: Blend (dry or wet) — feed beads
  Step 3: Ball mill (dry or wet) to produce slurry
  Step 4: Spray dry the slurry (parallel path)
  Step 5: Settle, decant, air dry, blend (parallel path)
  Step 6: Mix with PVC/CPVC dissolved in solvent
  Step 7: Apply membrane mix onto target surface
